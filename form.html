ABOUT

About SubFlow

SubFlow was developed as a capstone project for the Full Stack Development Laboratory course (23IT721). It showcases modern HTML5 semantic structure, responsive CSS layout, and JavaScript-driven interactivity — all deployed on Vercel with version control via GitHub.

The platform targets IT managers, finance teams, and startup founders who need a single source of truth for all subscription-based expenditures. With automated renewal alerts, cost forecasting, and SaaS auditing, SubFlow transforms how organizations manage their recurring software costs.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my subscriptions after registering?

Once you've submitted the registration form, you'll gain access to the SubFlow Dashboard where you can add, edit, and monitor all your active subscriptions in real time.

Is my personal data secure?

Absolutely. SubFlow uses industry-standard encryption and role-based access control to ensure your data is protected at all times.

Can I modify my registration details later?

Yes. After logging in, navigate to the Settings page where you can update your profile information, change your preferred meeting time, or modify your skills and interests.
